About the Club

The Mount Holyoke Entrepreneurship Club aims to inspire, teach, and connect Mount Holyoke women to become innovative business leaders and social entrepreneurs through workshops, speaker series, and conferences. We collaborate with the MHC Entreprenuership Programming , which hosts weekly lunch-ins as well as provides the resources on campus for budding entrepreneurs.

Do you have an idea for a social enterprise, a burning desire to solve a problem, an app idea?

You have come to the right place.

Events & Activities

What is Entrepreneurship? If you have a mind, and imagination, a passion for finding and filling needs, you are an entrepreneur. Entrepreneurship welcomes everyone with the drive and vision to recognize and create opportunity. (Babson College). Other than the annual Draper Competition and MHC Pitch Competition, we offer workshops on public speaking and speakers in the sectors of public service, tech, fashion, and more! For the 2015-2016 year, we are going bolder and better than ever before. If you would like to know more, join the mailing list.
